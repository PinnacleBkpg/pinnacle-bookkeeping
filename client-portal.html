What You Can Upload

Everything Goes Through the Portal

Keep your financial documents organized in one place. No more emailing attachments, no more hunting through your Downloads folder.

🧾

Receipts

Business expenses, fuel, meals, supplies, subscriptions. Snap a photo on your phone and upload directly.

📄

Invoices

Sales invoices you've issued to clients, as well as vendor invoices and bills payable to suppliers.

🏦

Bank Statements

Monthly statements for all business bank accounts and credit cards. PDF or CSV — both work.

👥

Payroll Documents

Timesheets, new hire information, employee changes, bonus approvals, and payroll-related records.

📝

Contracts & Agreements

Lease agreements, service contracts, loan documents, and any financial agreements affecting your books.

📬

CRA Correspondence

Any letters, assessments, or notices received from the CRA. Share them and we'll advise on next steps.

How It Works

Three Steps. No Technical Knowledge Required.

1

Log In

Access the secure portal with your login credentials provided during onboarding.

2

Upload

Drag and drop files or use the upload button. Name them clearly — or don't, we'll sort it.

3

Relax

We receive your documents, process them, and update your books. You'll be notified when done.

Your Financial Data Is Secure

We take the security of your financial information seriously. Our portal uses encrypted file transfer, and all documents are stored in compliance with PIPEDA — Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act.
