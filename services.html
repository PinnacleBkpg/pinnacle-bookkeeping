Get a Payroll Quote

Payroll is one of the most consequential admin tasks. One miscalculation or missed remittance and you're in trouble with the CRA.

  • Full payroll processing — weekly, bi-weekly, semi-monthly, or monthly
  • All statutory deductions — CPP, EI, and income tax
  • CRA payroll remittances (PD7A) prepared and filed on schedule
  • T4 slips and T4 Summary filed with the CRA
  • Record of Employment (ROE) preparation
  • Powered by QuickBooks Online Payroll, Wagepoint, or Powerpay
    • ⚠️

    Late Payroll Remittances Are Costly

    The CRA charges penalties of 3%–10% on late remittances — plus interest.
