Payroll Services

We run your payroll from start to finish — calculations, deductions, CRA remittances, and year-end T4s. You approve, we handle the rest.

Why it matters for your business

Accurate pay — every cycle, on time
CRA remittances never missed
T4s filed accurately and on time

Get a Payroll Quote

Payroll is one of the most time-consuming — and consequential — administrative tasks a business owner handles. One miscalculation, one missed remittance, and you're in trouble with the CRA. We eliminate that risk entirely.

  • Full payroll processing on your schedule — weekly, bi-weekly, semi-monthly, or monthly
  • Calculation of wages, salaries, bonuses, and statutory holiday pay
  • All statutory deductions — CPP, EI, and federal/provincial income tax
  • CRA payroll remittances (PD7A) prepared and filed on schedule
  • T4 slips and T4 Summary prepared and filed with the CRA
  • Record of Employment (ROE) preparation for departing employees
  • New hire reporting and compliance with Ontario's Employment Standards Act
  • Powered by QuickBooks Online Payroll, Wagepoint, or Powerpay
    • ⚠️

    Late Payroll Remittances Are Costly

    The CRA charges penalties of 3%–10% on late payroll remittances — plus interest. With Pinnacle managing your payroll, this never happens.

    🧾

    HST / GST Filing

    Preparation and filing of HST returns with the CRA. We calculate your ITC claims, net tax owing, and submit on time — monthly, quarterly, or annual depending on your filing period.

    Net tax calculation (ITC claims vs. HST collected)

    Filed on time — no late-filing penalties

    CRA liaison on HST matters when needed

    🏗️

    WSIB Filing

    Workplace Safety and Insurance Board filings for Ontario businesses. We calculate your insurable earnings, ensure your rate classification is correct, and file on time.

    🏥

    Employer Health Tax (EHT)

    Ontario EHT calculation and filing for businesses whose payroll exceeds the provincial exemption threshold. We calculate, remit, and file the annual return.
